Creates the .zip files of your themes and plugins and makes them ready to download.

== Description ==

Allows your to create the .zip files of the themes and plugins present on you WordPress site and gives you a direct link to download those files.
This can be used to export your themes and plugins to install them onto another website without having an ftp access to your site.
It can also be used to generate your initial .zip files of your themes and plugins once you've just finished building them.

== Installation ==

Upload the Sirmons Theme and Plugin Exporter plugin to your site, Activate it, and it's ready to use.
Once activated, a new submenu called "Sir plugin exporter" appears under the "Plugins" menu.
So go on the "Plugins->Sir plugin exporter" page, select the themes and plugins you wish to export and click on the "Create .zip files" button
and then click on the links that show up below to download your .zip files.
